Matt Shepard Is a Friend of Mine

About:

Production Company: Logo Documentary Films

Outlet: IDFA International Film Festival

Date: 10/2013–12/2013

Matt Shepard Is a Friend of Mine explores the life and death of Matthew Shepard, the gay student murdered in Laramie, Wyoming through a personal lens. His tragic case helped change Hate Crime Law in America. The film, by a first time director, was in the top 20 IDFA documentaries and later went on to win an Emmy.

Awards: Daytime Emmy
