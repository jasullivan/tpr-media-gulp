Children of Gaza

About:

Production Company: True Vison

Channel: Channel 4

Date: 01/2009–03/2009

In January 2009 over 1300 Palestinians were killed in Gaza, around 300 of them children. Children of Gaza is a documentary by BAFTA winning film-maker Jezza Neumann which follows three children in Gaza over a year to show the impact of war on their lives.

Awards: One World Media Award

Project:

tpr media coordinated a wide-ranging campaign designed to give an insight into the everyday lives of Palestinian children through features, TV and radio coverage together with comprehensive picks of the day.
