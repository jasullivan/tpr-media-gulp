The Mill

About:

Production Company: Darlow Smithson Productions

Channel: Channel 4

Date: 04/2013–07/2013 & 04/2014–07/2014

Based on historical archive, The Mill is a historical drama set in rural England at a time when the industrial revolution was changing the country beyond recognition. The story is told from the perspectives of young apprentices.

Project:

tpr media organised press screenings at the BFI in London and at Quarry Bank Mill in Cheshire, engaging with stories from its exhaustive National Trust archive. We also designed a media campaign which positioned the drama as a refreshing antidote to costume drama, and promoted its pool of young talent.
