Reuters-TIMA Penthouse Launch Party

previous next

Last night we were very excited to attend the penthouse party to launch the new Reuters-TIMA partnership and Location Services news facilities.

The new service, created in response to the increased demand for live content in a shifting media landscape, will form a vast news and editorial network providing every aspect of newsgathering facilities and support services.

The UK and international news industry gathered to celebrate the launch in the penthouse suite of New Zealand House (TIMA’s London HQ) overlooking Trafalgar Square. It was a wonderful event with delicious canapes, plenty of enthusiasm and panaramic views of the city!

Guests even had the opportunity to record their own news broadcast, choosing between real Reuters-TIMA city backdrops set against the skylines of London, Paris, New York or Rio. tpr’s Eleanor took full advantage of, enjoying the chance to get in front of the camera.

To learn more about the new Reuters-TIMA Location Services set up to cover every major world event, from elections to summits, sports to national celebrations and breaking news such as war, natural disasters and militant attacks, visit www.reuters-tima.com
